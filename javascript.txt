Your future colleagues
OneTick/Core Technology/IBIT team provides statistical and quantitative analytics for all the major markets across the globe for all the asset classes (Equities/Rates/FX). We capture and store real time and well as historical transactional data and market data. This is used to provide analytics to business users e.g. pre/post trade analytics, back testing quant strategies, volatility, spread computation, Alpha progression, Global Curve plotting, heatmap analysis and so on. OneTick team provides good blend of technology and analytics development work. OneTick's analytical engine is a stream oriented framework for efficient computations based on time series data.
